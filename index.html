What is a Frontier Firm?

A Frontier Firm is a human-led, AI-operated organization. Humans set strategic intent, maintain values, and make judgment calls — while AI agents handle execution at scale. It's not about replacing people; it's about unlocking human potential with AI.

1

Foundation

Human with Assistant

AI augments individual work. People use Copilot to draft, summarize, analyze, and create — building trust and developing AI fluency across the organization.

What this looks like:
• Copilot helps draft emails and meeting summaries
• Teams use AI for document creation and data analysis
• Basic AI governance and acceptable use policies in place
2

Expansion

Human-Agent Teams

AI agents handle parts of workflows while humans review and approve. Teams begin working alongside agents, with defined decision rights and oversight structures.

What this looks like:
• AI agents triage support tickets and draft responses
• Agents generate financial reports with human review
• Skills-based roles emerge; AI is part of team OKRs
3

Frontier

Human-Led, Agent-Operated

Agents run entire workflows end-to-end. Humans manage intent, set guardrails, handle exceptions, and maintain strategic oversight — this is the Frontier Firm.

What this looks like:
• Agent orchestration handles multi-step processes
• Board-level AI governance and continuous optimization
• "Work Chart" model: agents are part of the org structure
